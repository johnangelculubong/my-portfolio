My journey into design and development started with curiosity. I've always been fascinated by how websites and apps solve problems and make life easier. As I kept learning and experimenting, I realized I enjoy building things that don't just work, but also feel smooth and intuitive to use. I'm still learning and growing, but this creative process is what makes me excited to keep pushing forward.
I'm on a journey to become a UI/UX Designer and Web Developer, driven by a love for design and a desire to solve real-world problems. I'm still learning and honing my skills, but I enjoy every part of the process — from sketching ideas to building them into real, functional projects. My goal is to create digital experiences that connect with people and make their online interactions smoother and more enjoyable.
I get inspired by clean and simple designs, creative websites, and how small details can make a big difference in how people experience something online. I also find a lot of ideas from the apps I use, cool UI in games, and even just everyday tech interactions. These little moments push me to keep learning and creating stuff that feels useful and easy for people.
